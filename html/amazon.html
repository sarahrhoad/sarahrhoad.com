Amazon Music Banner Ad

The Initial Process

To gain more experience my design mentor suggested I find media ads to recreate and develop using HTML and CSS.

First I replicated the ads as close to the originals as possible. After that I redesigned each ad for an extra challenge, then built both versions using HTML and CSS.

In doing so it has helped me gain a better understanding of what design elements are used when creating ads, blogs, websites, etc.

I also have a new appreciation for the different ways space, color, shapes, imagery, etc. play a role in the look, feel, and voice of a message or website.

The Final Draft

The Amazon banner ad was the first media ad I completed. I created all of the color overlays, as well as the Amazon logo from scratch.

My two biggest challenges I found during the design process were trying to match the various color splotches and accurately creating the Amazon logo.
