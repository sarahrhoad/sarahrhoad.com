Medium Sign In Page

The Initial Process

My design mentor advised me to find different media ads to redesign and then develop on the web.

In doing so it has helped me gain a better understanding of what design elements are used when creating ads, blogs, websites, etc.

I have a new appreciation for the different ways space, color, shapes, imagery, etc. play a role in the look, feel, and voice of a message or website.

This media source is a sign-in popup where users either sign into an exisiting account or create a new account.

Website Ad Campaign

The media website's main focus is on presenting the news stories they are publishing. They use a lot of white space throughout their website. Saving the color and design for the images found within the articles.

For this reason I chose to have a smaller images displayed in a unique way, with more transparent, and artistic graphical design elements in the background to add a little depth to the page.

I also dampened the images to help keep the users focus on either signing in or creating an account since that is the main purpose for this screen.
