Fitness Coupon

The Initial Process

My design mentor advised me to find different media ads to copy and then develop on the web.

In doing so it has helped me gain a better understanding of what design elements are used when creating ads, blogs, websites, etc.

I have a new appreciation for the different ways space, color, shapes, imagery, etc. play a role in the look, feel, and voice of a message or website.

Recreating this ad was not difficult due to everything being center aligned and the majority of the items contained in boxes or buttons.

Fitness Coupon Redesign

For the redesign I chose a neutral image so as not to exclude anyone.

I removed some of the veribiage to make it clearer that users need to sign up in order to save 5% on their order.

I also moved the fine print to the bottom since the point of the popup is first and foremost for a user to sign up. So to make the phone number and sign up buttons more of the focal point I have them in the middle of the add.
